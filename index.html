The price of a single visa for travel to Dubai for up to 14 days is USD 150. If you need to stay between 14 and 30 days, the price is USD 190, and for 30 to 60 days, it is USD 350. All these visas are for a single entry to Dubai. There is also a super express visa that takes 24 business hours and has a supplement of USD 150. All our prices are final.
